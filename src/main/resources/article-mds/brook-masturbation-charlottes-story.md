# Masturbation: Charlotte's story

Charlotte, 20, shares her journey of becoming comfortable with masturbation, overcoming childhood shame, and understanding what pleasure means to her.

**Content warning:** This page contains discussion of homophobia.

## Early Discoveries and Sensations

Discovery of pleasure often begins before an individual understands the terminology associated with it. For Charlotte, masturbation became a regular activity throughout adolescence once she realized that touching her vulva created pleasurable sensations.

This period of discovery often involves:
* Exploring physical sensations through everyday objects.
* Identifying specific environments, such as a bath or shower, that enhance pleasure.
* Recognizing that certain visual media can trigger physical responses.

## Understanding Sexuality and Peer Influence

Growing up, many young people learn about sex and sexuality through playground talk or overheard adult conversations. Charlotte recalls being exposed to terms like "lesbian" and "sex" at a young age without fully understanding their meaning, often accompanied by a sense of confusion or social stigma.

Her first exposure to pornography occurred at age nine through a friend's computer. This experience was characterized by a mix of:
* Intense fascination and curiosity.
* Intrigue regarding adult genitals.
* Panic and the fear of being caught.

## Overcoming Shame and Stigma

At age 13, Charlotte began using the internet to explore her sexuality, gravitating toward lesbian content. However, when her father discovered she was watching porn at age 14, the resulting disciplinary action led to deep feelings of shame and disgust.

For several years, this shame transformed masturbation into a functional act—"to scratch an itch"—rather than an act of self-love or exploration. This highlights how childhood shame and negative reinforcement from authority figures can dominate a person's sexual journey well into adulthood.

## Building Confidence and Self-Worth

Between the ages of 18 and 21, living independently allowed Charlotte to reclaim her relationship with her body. This transition included:
* **Exploring Sex Toys:** Buying a first vibrator and understanding the variety of purposes for sex toys.
* **Open Discussion:** Creating platforms like "Don't Say Sex" to discuss masturbation and sexual health openly.
* **Self-Acceptance:** Learning to dissociate shame from masturbation and recognizing it as a healthy, positive activity.

## The Benefits of Masturbation

Through her journey, Charlotte has identified several ways masturbation contributes to overall wellbeing:
* **Body Appreciation:** Understanding and loving one’s own body.
* **Sexual Confidence:** Better communication with partners regarding likes, dislikes, and boundaries.
* **Self-Discovery:** A lifelong journey of experimentation where techniques and preferences continue to evolve.
* **Sexual Health:** Understanding personal sexuality and physical boundaries.

## Legal Information for Under 18s (UK)

It is important to understand the legal landscape regarding sexual content and products in the UK:
* **Pornography:** It is not against the law to watch porn if you are under 18, but it is illegal to buy it. It is also illegal to show pornography to anyone under the age of 18.
* **Sex Toys:** It is illegal to purchase sex toys if you are under the age of 18.
