# Meeting a Partner Online: Fiona's Story

Fiona, 21, shares how technology played a vital role in her relationship, from their initial meeting on WhatsApp to maintaining a long-distance connection over several years.

## The First Connection

After a difficult period in her personal life, Fiona was hesitant about dating. Her path changed when a friend created a large WhatsApp group for a school project.

* **Initial Attraction:** Fiona noticed a contact in the group and was intrigued by his profile photo.
* **The Matchmaker:** A mutual friend acted as an intermediary, sharing Fiona’s photo and description with him.
* **First Contact:** He reached out via message, leading to three months of digital communication before they were able to meet in person.

## Navigating the Early Stages

Because of logistical challenges—including age, the inability to drive, and university commitments—their relationship began entirely online.

* **Building a Foundation:** For the first few months, they were "purely friends," using the time to get to know one another.
* **Digital Flirting:** Despite some initial uncertainty about his interest, they eventually began flirting through text.
* **Official Relationship:** Six months after their first message, they officially became a couple.

## Overcoming Distance Through Technology

The relationship faced a significant challenge when Fiona moved to Scotland for university only two months after they started dating. Technology became the primary tool for maintaining their bond.

* **Long-Distance Commitment:** Despite the challenges of long distance, they decided to stay together and have now been long-distance for nearly three years.
* **The Role of Gaming:** They use online games to bond and share experiences regardless of the physical distance between them.
* **Digital Communication Tools:** The couple uses Discord to chat daily, keeping the connection alive.

## Tools for Staying Connected

Technology has provided various solutions to the practical and emotional hurdles of a long-distance relationship:

* **Communication Platforms:** Using Discord for daily conversations and video calls.
* **Collaborative Activities:** Playing online games together to create shared memories.
* **Logistics and Gifts:** Using mobile apps to book plane tickets and services like Amazon to send gifts.
* **Technical Bonding:** Fiona even learned to build her first PC with remote instructions from her partner’s brother.
* **Sensory Technology:** Exploring options like "bond touch" bracelets, which allow partners to send physical vibrations to each other remotely.

Fiona credits technology with making her relationship possible, stating that it allows them to function as a "normal" couple despite living in different countries.
