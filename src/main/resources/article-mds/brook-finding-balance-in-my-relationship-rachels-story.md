# Finding Balance in My Relationship: Rachel’s Story

Rachel, 19, explains why prioritising time for yourself when you’re in a relationship is essential. She shares how investing energy into self-growth has allowed both her and her relationship to flourish.

## Prioritising the Relationship with Yourself
For years, societal narratives have often suggested that women exist primarily to please others. Rachel decided to challenge this narrative by starting a "relationship with herself." After being in a romantic relationship for over five years, she realised that finding a balance between her individual needs and her partner's needs was a top priority. 

Key realisations included:
* You are the only person who can truly fulfil your own needs.
* Depending entirely on someone else to meet all your desires is unsustainable.
* Learning to rely on yourself is an evolving process that grows over time.

## Shifting Perspectives on Partners
Rachel and her boyfriend began dating at age 14. Initially, she felt conditioned to believe that a partner should be her "everything" and satisfy every wish. As the couple matured, they realised this dynamic was not healthy. They began implementing small changes to ensure they were living for themselves as individuals, not just as a unit.

Practical changes they implemented:
* **Communicating availability:** Letting each other know when they have individual plans or hobbies.
* **Scheduled time apart:** Adding at least two days into their weekly routine where they spend time completely separate from one another.
* **The "Me First" Motto:** Operating under the principle of "me first, us second."

## The Importance of Boundaries
Setting boundaries is a vital part of self-love. It involves taking pride in personal limits and clearly communicating them to others. By implementing days for themselves, Rachel and her partner ensure they have the space to grow independently while still enjoying their time together.

## Self-Growth and Personal Milestones
During the COVID-19 lockdown, physical separation provided Rachel with an opportunity to focus intensely on her own desires. She discovered that investing time in herself, rather than trying to satisfy someone else's needs, allowed her self-growth to flourish.

A major milestone during this time was the creation of her sex-positive Instagram account. This project allowed her to pour energy into a cause that benefited both herself and others, marking a significant step in her journey of self-discovery.

## Practicing Self-Love Through "Solo Dates"
Rachel believes that everyone deserves "date nights" with themselves. For her, this often involves:
* Taking herself to a coffee shop to work or relax.
* Practicing the belief that she is worthy of time and self-love.
* Navigating the initial nerves or embarrassment of being out alone.
* Reminding herself that others are generally not judging her actions.

## Mutual Support and Individual Success
A healthy relationship should allow both individuals to navigate their own lives while enjoying their love together. Rachel credits her partner's support for helping her understand her own worth and her ability to reach her aspirations. Ultimately, while relationships with others are valuable, the relationship you have with yourself is the most important one.
